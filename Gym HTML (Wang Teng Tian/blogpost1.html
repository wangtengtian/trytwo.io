BEGINNER TOOLS

Author: Wang Teng Tian

27/01/2021 at 11:59 pm

Just starting off as a beginner and seeking content to learn but don't know where to find? You may have talked with your friends who are self-proclaimed experts that give misleading advice. This hinders with your fitness journey. Who can I trust? What can I use? In this blog, I will share some reliable sources that you can learn from as a beginner.

1. Athlean-X

Athlean-X is one of the most renowned fitness channels globally and has 7.8 million subscribers. They have trained Aamir Khan and other celebrities as well. Over here, there are free workouts, nutrition and supplement advice as well the do's and don'ts of weight training.
Click here to visit their Youtube channel

2. Bar Brothers

The channel was started by two Russian guys (Lazar Novovic and Dusan Djolevic). The content revolves on Calisthenics workout and natural body building. Calisthenics is very convenient to carry out as you only need your body-weight for training. Bar Brothers have become a global family whereby many countries have their own Bar Brothers community, encouraging each other to do street workout. The Bar Brothers' transformation journey is incredibly inspiring.
