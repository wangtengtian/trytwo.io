FAQ

Q1. Can I not sign a contract and pay by per visit?

Yes you may! The rates are as follows:

  • Singapore locations S$53.50 w/GST per visit
  • Shanghai locations ¥350 per visit
  • Hong Kong locations HK$35.50 per visit

For any enquiries, please call the centres hotline/email which can be found on the Contact page.

Q2. I don't see a plan that suit my needs, can I customise?

Yes, you may! You may contact us to make an appointment and our advisors will work with you on a suitable plan that caters for you.
Do take note that the price of customisation is based on these factors:

  • The number of locations being accessed – All-location access being the most expensive
  • Visit/Class restrictions within location
  • Length of the contract
  • Pre-paid contract vs. monthly payment plan
  • Renewal of an existing contract vs. all-new contract
  • Corporate affiliations
Q3. What happens if I want to cancel my plan?
  1. If you hold a Monthly Committed Autopay Package, you may not terminate your Agreement until the full minimum term has expired. After such time you may terminate your Agreement by completing a Termination Form at your centre or studio on or before:
    i) the 5th day of the month if your Autopay date is on the 16th day of each month;
    (ii) the 20th day of the month if your Autopay date is on the 1st day of each month;
    Your Agreement will then expire one (1) month after the following 1st or 16th bill cycle. If we have not received your termination request in writing, your Agreement will automatically continue after the minimum term period by billing you via Autopay each month;e

  2. If your request to terminate is given after the dates indicated in Clause 1 above, you will be charged one (1) month's non-refundable Monthly Fee, and your rights and privilleges under your Agreement shall expire two (2) months after the last Autopay deduction;

  3. If you hold a Prepaid Package, you may not terminate your Agreement within the Prepaid Period;

  4. If you hold a Prepaid Package, you may not terminate your Agreement within the Prepaid Period;

  5. Termination of your Agreement will be automatically lead to termination of locker rental on the same date

  6. Termination of your Agreement will be automatically lead to termination of locker rental on the same date
