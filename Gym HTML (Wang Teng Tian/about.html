About Us

In December 2003, we opened our first Pure Fitness gym in Kinwick Centre (Central). Over the years, we have established our fitness presence in Hong Kong and expanded into Singapore and Shanghai. We are dedicated to helping people to lead healthier and happier lives.

It is our duty to serve the community of healthy and active living, by bringing the best fitness experiences from around the world.

Our Mission

We have an intense obssession in providing the best servives possible. We are always sourcign for the lastest and adavanced equipments such as Technogym, LifeFitness, Jacobes Ladder and more.

Our teams are specially selected for their wide experiences and skillsets that cannot be found easily. Find out more below.
